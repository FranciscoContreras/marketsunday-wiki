## The six jobs

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Courier" icon="random">
		Hauls items from one container to another. The workhorse of any storage room —
		hand it a route and forget about it.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Stoker" icon="setting">
		Keeps a furnace fed: fuel to the fuel slot, ore to the input. Smelting lines
		that never go cold.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Restocker" icon="add-document">
		Tops a container up to a set amount. Perfect for shop chests, brewing supplies,
		and the community bread barrel.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Janitor" icon="approve-check">
		Vacuums up dropped items nearby and banks them in a chest. No more inventory
		confetti after a creeper visit.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Sorter" icon="list-format">
		Splits one chest across many, matching items to the chest that already holds
		their kind. A tidy warehouse, on legs.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Farmhand" icon="star">
		Harvests and replants the crops around it. Pair one with a Janitor and the
		farm runs itself.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
